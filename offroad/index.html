Package Options and What to bring

If you are looking for an all day off-road adventure then you have come to the right place! We offer guided off-road trips through the famous trails in Moab, Utah. Each trip is customized by you! Choose from trip packs we have made that vary in trip length, number of trails, and difficulty, or customize your own trip by selecting each of these separately!

We have three vehicle options to choose from, all of which are more than capable to handle anything that gets thrown at them! Choose from a Jeep, Polaris rzr, or a Can-am Maverik X3. You must be 21 years old or older to operate any of these vehicle and have a valid drivers license. Listed below are pictures of each vehicle along with their specs!

All off-roading gear and equipment is provided to you for the length of the trip! All you need to bring is your own food, water, and appropriate clothing for the weather! This all-inclusive experience will make you want to come back year after year!
